rylo

Rylo is a powerful 360° camera that uses advanced software to create high-quality, cinematic videos in 5.8k resolution. It captures everything around you smoothly with great stabilization

After filming, just connect Rylo to your smartphone and use the Rylo app to quickly edit and create a video you'll love to share

iOS App

version 1.6.2 (the latest version)

Features

Export in 360 Equirectangular: the app will export the 360 video to your phones camera roll, where you can upload direction to Facebook/YouTube or transfer over to a desktop for further editing

Tiny Planets: the new app will allow you to create tiny planets from your photos/videos by pinching outwards

Image Tuning: tune your images after they have been shot. You’ll be able to adjust light, contrast, highlights, shadows and vibrancy

Low Light Improvement: improves the low light shooting capabilities of the Rylo
